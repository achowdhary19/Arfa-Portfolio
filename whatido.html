What I do

I'm a creative technologist interested in working between digital and physical mediums. I work at the intersection of design, programming, and strategy to create intelligent interactive systems.

As a designer

I design interfaces that are personalized and functional. My design process is an iterative process that includes research, strategizing, and protopying. This works best and is most enjoyable when it is collaborative, engaged, process between me and you. My strengths lie in my relationships with people as I'm great collaborator and help you develop a clear shape for your ideas. I enjoy working with important stories, enthusiastic people, and strong communicators.

As a programmer

I build small-to-medium experiences from concept to launch. My systems prioritize function, highlight your body work in the most fitting way through differnet types of media, and uphold accessibility standards. Im experineced with a variety of web building softwares, but I enjoy building with minimal tech - HTML, CSS, and JS - when the project fits.
This website is handcoded and maintained by me, with love.

As a game designer

I develop games and experimental media using Unity, JavaScript, and I experiment with alternative engines like Pico-8 and Twine. I'm experienced with AR development and creating small-scale multi media applications.

As a maker

While I’m often working with digital design, I also work with Arduino, soldering, metalworking, 3D modeling + printing, and laser cutting.
