Tiny Games: Garden Game

Click the image to check it out!

These games are tiny because they are sketchy and they are made within the tight technical constraints of their custom hardware. These games are made using puzzlescript, pico-8, and bitsy.

Bitsy is another small editor that supports more narrative games. Bitsy games can have multiple rooms where sprites can talk to eachother and collect items. In my garden game(currently unfinished), the player runs around collecting vegetables to bring back to the house and make soup with.
