Tiny Games: Angels

These games are tiny because they are sketchy and they are made within the tight technical constraints of their custom hardware. These games are made using puzzlescript, pico-8, and bitsy.

Pico-8 is the most powerful game engine in the bunch. Because Pico-8 uses Lua, the games can be much more filled out and complicated as Lua supports object-oriented programming. Angels (currently unfinished), is a butterfly catching game.
