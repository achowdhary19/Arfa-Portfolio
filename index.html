Dollhouse extends traditional, tabletop play by allowing house guests to play in a physical dollhouse and teleport to a virtual, video game dollhouse. Using Unity, Arduino, and RFID technology.

Work from my time with Providence Health and Services where I helped build a web and mobile application to host immersive, 3D videos. Using html, css, javascript, A-frame, and 8th wall

Oblivion is a 3D mobile puzzle game currently in early development by Unity Production Foundations. Set in historically rich environments, the game challenges players to solve complex puzzles using tools build in the Middle Ages.

Orcas Microcinema is a nonprofit itinerant microcinema based in Orcas Island, Washington and opening in August 2024.

Smelling rain (WIP) is a narrative flatgame made in Unity.

Looking for... (WIP) is an interactive poetry site. Made with html, css, and javascript.

Lets Play is a world full of easter eggs. Made with html, css, javascript, and images of items in my room.

8 Ball is a tiny game made within the tight technical constraints of its custom hardware, Puzzlescript.

Angels (WIP) is a butterfly catching game made in Pico-8.
