Hi, my name is Arfa. I’m a designer and programmer interested in creating tools that encourage curiosity and deeper engagement with information, environments, and culture. Read more about my skills and interests here.

→ guestbook

Designing and developing a spatial data visualization tool for Ecolibrium at the Loisaida Center. Investigating how energy infrastructure, environmental conditions, and urban systems interact at a neighborhood scale.

The Labyrinth is a narrative web experience I designed for Hyundai Artlab's commission by artist Kyung-Me, using motion, pacing, and interaction to signal internal transformation.

Providence's is an AR-based physical therapy tool I designed the interface and interaction system for, letting patients view and learn exercises in their own space through volumetric video.

Vanished is a 3D escape room game by Unity Production Foundations, set in historically rich environments and challenging players to solve puzzles using tools built in the Middle Ages. I contributed UX research and playtesting during development.

AR Worldcraft is a game design and development course taught at NuVu innovation school.

Orcas Microcinema is a nonprofit itinerant cinema in Washington. I designed and developed its website to serve as a public-facing archive of screenings and support the organization's collaboration and funding efforts.

Cafe Mornings is a café whose website I redesigned to improve navigation and user experience.

Espacio Enredo is an independent contemporary art space and publishing initiative in Madrid. I designed and developed its website to archive exhibitions and publications and support outreach through newsletters and press materials.

Smelling rain (WIP) is a narrative flatgame made in Unity.

Looking for... (WIP) is an handmade words site made with HTML/CSS/JS.
